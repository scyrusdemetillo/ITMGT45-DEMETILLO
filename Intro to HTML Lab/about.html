Our Story

Founded in 2021, Digital Espresso began as a hobby for our its founder, Scyrus Demetillo, who enjoyed making and perfecting coffee at home. After doing countless internships and mentorship programs under the best coffee makers in the country, Scyrus put up Digital Espresso as a small kiosk shop at Gyud Food Hub for students and workers alike to enjoy. Now they have expanded to small shops in malls across Metro Manila, continuing to bring the best and highest quality coffee.

"To craft the best coffee expereince that brings the fun and flavor to people worldwide"

Our Coffee Philosophy

We source only the top 2% of Arabica beans from sustainable farms across Latin America and Southeast Asia. Our proprietary roasting process, developed over three years of experimentation, brings out the most amount of flavor and soothing comfort from every ingredient.

To be qualified, every barista completes our intensive hands-on training program covering all aspects of coffee making and designing. To ensure we deliver the best quality of drinks, our machinery and materials are made from the highest quality and by the best producers in the world.

Community Commitment

With our aim to be more sustainable, we've eliminated single-use plastics from all locations. Instead, all our takeaway cups are 100% compostable and our straws are plant-based that are edible after long-uses. Moreover, all our branches are powered by both solar and electricity to push for a more greener use of resources.
