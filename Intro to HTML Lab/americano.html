Our Signature Americano

Classic Americano

190

Our Americano is crafted with a double shot of our premium espresso blended with hot water to create a rich, smooth coffee with a delightful crema. Perfect for those who enjoy a stronger coffee experience without the intensity of straight espresso. Served in a 12oz cup with optional milk or sweeteners on the side.

Ingredients: Espresso, Hot Water

Serving Size: 12oz

Calories: 10 (without additions)

Caffeine: 150mg
