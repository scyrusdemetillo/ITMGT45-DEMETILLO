Our Signature Latte

Latte

190

Our signature latte combines rich espresso with velvety steamed milk, creating a smooth and comforting coffee experience. The perfect balance of one-third espresso to two-thirds steamed milk, topped with a light layer of microfoam. Available in 12oz or 16oz sizes, hot or iced.

Ingredients: Espresso, Steamed Milk, Milk Foam

Serving Size: 12oz (355ml)

Calories: 190 (with whole milk)

Caffeine: 150mg

Customizations: Available with whole, skim, almond, oat or soy milk
